Peaks of mountain

A mountain is a prominent landform that rises significantly above its surroundings. It often has steep slopes, a small peak area, and significant local relief. Although hills are usually thought to be smaller than mountains, there is no set definition for the term in geology.

Hiking for peoples

The reasons hiking is great for the whole body. Climbing and descending hills increases heart rate and provides an excellent cardiac exercise. Hiking, like most aerobic exercises, lowers your risk of high blood pressure, high cholesterol, heart disease, stroke, and even some malignancies.
